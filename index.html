About Alamanda College

Alamanda College has been up and running since 2013. Today it has over 3500 kids and is one of the biggest schools in Victoria. It has many anual events such as tennis, swimming and the downball competition, and has tons of extra-cirriculur activites such as physical education, art and languages other than english.
